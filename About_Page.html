This Website was created by Ryan Dreher for Web Design (Comp 20) at Tufts University. The purpose of this website is to allow for contact a plumber directly via FaceTime or Skype so that the plumber may talk to their clients remotely. My Uncle, who is a plumber, said one of the biggest pains in his job is driving two hours to a house where problem is fixed in ten minutes. This is a proof of concept to solve my Uncle's Issues.
